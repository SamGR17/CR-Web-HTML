Fun Facts about Barn Owls

They are silent when they fly

Their silent flying helps them to sneak up on unsuspecting prey and hear their sounds.

They have uneven ears

Their ears are asymmetrical, one ear is slightly higher than the other, but this actually helps them hear better!

They have low-light vision

This ability lets them see in the dark well and find prey during the night by sight.
