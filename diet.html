A Barn Owl's Diet

Barn owls eat a variety of small animals. Barn owls usually eat small mammals such as mice, rats, voles, and shrews. Barn owls also occasionally eat birds, amphibians, reptiles, insects, and invertebrates.

Barn owls mostly eat voles


This is a vole.

Voles make up 45% of a barn owl's diet for those living in mainland Britain, as it is the most common food for them.
