Barn Owls

This is a barn owl. Their scientific name is Tyto alba.

It is the most widely distributed species of owl in the world and one of the most widespread of all species of birds.

They live around agricultural areas, basalt cliffs, forest openings, wetlands, and other open spaces in the wild. They can be found on every continent except Antarctica.

I like Barn Owls, because they are cool and cute, even if their screech sounds like a banshee.
